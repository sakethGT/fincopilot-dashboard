## 📖 Plain English Glossary

> [!info]- Every technical term used today — click to expand

| Term | What It Actually Means |
|---|---|
| **P/E Ratio** | Price-to-Earnings. You pay $X for every $1 of company profit. Lower can mean cheaper — but compare within the same sector. |
| **Yield Curve** | A chart of interest rates across different loan lengths (1yr to 30yr). Normal = long rates higher than short. Inverted = short > long = recession warning. Today's curve is normal (+0.46% spread). |
| **Fear & Greed Index** | 0–100 score of market emotion. 0–25 = Extreme Fear (historically a buying zone). 75–100 = Extreme Greed (be cautious). Today: 13 = Extreme Fear. |
| **DCA** | Dollar-Cost Averaging — invest a fixed amount every month regardless of price. Removes the stress of timing the market. You automatically buy more shares when prices are low. |
| **SIP** | Systematic Investment Plan — India's version of DCA. Auto-debit fixed INR monthly into a mutual fund. |
| **Liquidations** | When leveraged crypto traders get forced out of their positions because the market moved against them. $300M in BTC longs liquidated = a lot of forced sellers hitting the market at once. Often signals a short-term bottom. |
| **BTC Dominance** | Bitcoin's % of total crypto market value. High (~55%+) = investors prefer safety of BTC over riskier altcoins. Today: ~55% = mild flight to BTC quality. |
| **Strait of Hormuz** | A narrow waterway between Iran and Oman. ~20% of global oil supply passes through it daily. If Iran blocks it (as threatened during conflicts), oil prices spike globally. |
| **Equity Risk Premium** | Extra return investors demand for owning stocks vs. risk-free bonds. India's ERP = 7.08% vs US = 4.46%. Higher = more risky but more expected return. |
| **NRE Account** | Non-Resident External. You deposit foreign earnings here in INR. Interest is fully tax-free in India. You can move money back to the US with no restrictions. |
| **Repatriation** | Moving investment gains from India back to the US. NRE accounts allow full repatriation; NRO accounts cap it at $1M/year. |

*Additional terms used today:*

| Term | What It Actually Means |
|---|---|
| **Capitulation** | When investors give up and sell everything out of fear, regardless of value. Marks the emotional bottom of a selloff. The $300M BTC liquidation today may be partial capitulation. |
| **Soft Landing** | When a central bank raises rates to fight inflation but avoids causing a recession. GDP +1.11% QoQ suggests the US is achieving this. |
